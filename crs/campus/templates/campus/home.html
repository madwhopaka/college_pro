About Us

Training and Placement Cell is an integral part of this institute. Training activities are organized throughout the year in an effort towards preparing the prospective students for the campus selection. Training and Placements of students is the primary function of the department. It also takes initiative for developing right attitudes, soft skills, motivating the students to have strong technical as well as analytical capabilities and exposing them to the requirements of corporate world and helping them in their professional careers.
